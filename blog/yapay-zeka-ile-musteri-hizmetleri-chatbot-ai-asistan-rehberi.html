Chatbots fall into two main categories. Choosing the right type for your business needs is critical.

  • AI chatbot (LLM-powered): Understands natural language, gives contextual answers. Solves complex queries, learns, personalizes.
  • Hybrid model: Auto-answers FAQs, escalates complex issues to human agents. Most practical for SMBs.
  • Voice AI assistant: Answers phone calls automatically. Handles appointments, order status by voice.

  • 💰 Price & stock info: "Is product X in stock? Price?" → Instant answer from database.
  • 📅 Appointment management: "Can I book for 2 PM tomorrow?" → Checks calendar, creates booking if available.

  • Average response time: Time from customer message to bot reply (target: <5 sec).
